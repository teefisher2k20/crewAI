## 2025-05-15 - [Consistent Post-Creation Guidance]
**Learning:** Users often feel "lost" after a CLI creates a new project structure. Providing a clearly formatted "Next Steps" panel with the exact commands needed to proceed (navigation, installation, execution) significantly improves the onboarding experience and reduces cognitive load.
**Action:** Always include a visual confirmation of success followed by actionable next steps for any command that generates files or project structures.
